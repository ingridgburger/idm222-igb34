Patchwork Zine

By undergrads, for undergrads.

Our vision is to weave together established and unsung voices of students, stitching them into a dynamic and energizing patchwork inspired by the 90s zine movement. Each issue is centered around a theme, meant to serve as a creative anchor to guide and inspire submitters.
